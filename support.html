Support

We’re here to help.

Getting Help

If you’re having trouble with RankSpinnah or have questions about upcoming apps like ComicSpinnah, we’re happy to help. Spinnah is a one-person studio, so responses are personal, not automated.

Contact Channels

You can reach out using any of the following:

  • Email: info@spinnah.app
  • Twitter / X: @ShastLeLow
  • GitHub: github.com/spinnah

Typical response time is within 1–2 business days, though during busy release windows it may take a bit longer.

Reporting a Bug

When reporting a bug, it helps a lot if you can include:

  • Which app and platform (e.g., RankSpinnah on macOS 15, iOS 18, etc.)
  • What you were trying to do
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened (including any error messages)
  • Screenshots if you’re comfortable sharing them

Feature Requests

Have an idea that would make a Spinnah app more useful, more fun, or just less annoying? Send it along — real-world usage is a big part of how future updates are prioritized.

While not every request can make it into the apps, thoughtful feedback is always appreciated.
