Our Philosophy

How we build — and why we build this way.

Built the Way Apps Should Be

We’re wicked tired of the subscription plague. And the “buy it on iPhone then buy it again on Mac” scam? Miss us with that.

Spinnah apps are native, fast, private, and built with intention — no ads, no tracking, and no web wrappers pretending to be software.

Honest Pricing

If a Spinnah app works on multiple Apple platforms, you buy it once. Period. Some apps start on one platform because that’s where they make the most sense — but if an iOS app ever grows into a Mac app down the road? You’re already covered.

No second purchase. No double dipping. No nonsense.

No Subscriptions for Basic Features

We believe software should respect your time, attention, and wallet. Spinnah apps avoid subscriptions unless there is a real, unavoidable, ongoing cost to operate a feature. So far? We haven’t needed one.

Made by Someone Who Actually Uses This Stuff

Spinnah is a one-person, passion-driven studio — not a factory. Everything is built with care, tested for real workflows, and designed around the kind of user experience we wish more apps had.

Good software, made honestly. That’s the whole deal.
