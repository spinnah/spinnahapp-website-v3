Privacy Policy

Effective November 2025

Spinnah apps are designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect personal information, analytics data, or tracking identifiers. Your data remains on your devices and, if applicable, within your iCloud account under your control.

For apps that integrate with iCloud or Apple frameworks, we use only Apple-provided APIs to store and sync user content securely. We do not operate any external servers, nor do we access or share your information with third parties.

If you have any questions about how your data is handled, please reach out at info@spinnah.app.
